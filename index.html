About

The Alien Runner is a platform game more focused on an endless runner experience with levels. The theme is you are an Alien in the Outer Space collecting valuables but constantly getting attacked by two hostile mobs. There will be 6 levels Currently, where each level has its own speciality.

FAQ

Either you should use your spacebar key to jump from the obstacles and obtain the valuables or when given, you can use the jump button which is located just below the alien. At further levels, you would be provided with a duck-in button to duck from the obstacles


There are two obstacles, the Skeleton and the demon. As you collect your valuables, the demon's purpose is to steel it from you and the skeleton would finish you.


The game is completely created using javascript and displayed using the Canvas tag. Yes it uses a library. The library used is p5.play.js
